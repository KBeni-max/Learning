Gollum

About Gollum

Gollum, also known as Sméagol, is a fascinating character from J.R.R. Tolkien's "The Lord of the Rings." Once a hobbit-like creature, Gollum's obsession with the One Ring transformed him into a wretched and pitiful being.

Gollum and his looks

Gollum's traits:

  • Emaciated physique: Gollum's body is frail and undernourished.
  • Stealthy and cunning: Gollum possesses exceptional sneakiness and cleverness.
  • Obsessively possessive: Gollum is consumed by an insatiable desire to possess the One Ring.
  • Split personality: Gollum's mind is divided between Sméagol and a malevolent alter ego.
  • Tragic figure: Gollum's pitiable nature stems from his tormented past and current state.
